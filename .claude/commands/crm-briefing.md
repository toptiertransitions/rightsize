Use the rightsize MCP tools to generate a daily CRM briefing.

Steps:
1. Call `crm_dashboard` to get the full overview.
2. Present the results in this format:

---
## CRM Briefing — [today's date]

### Pipeline
[List open opportunities by stage with counts. Call out any overdue next steps in bold.]

### Due This Week
[List opportunities with next steps due in the next 7 days: client name, stage, what the next step is, and due date.]

### Referral Partners Needing Follow-Up
[List referral contacts with no recent activity — name, company, stage, last activity date. Flag as "overdue" if > 30 days.]

### Active Referral Companies ([count])
[Brief list of companies currently sending referrals.]

### Activity Last 7 Days
[Breakdown by type: Calls, Emails, Meetings, Notes, Tasks.]
---

Keep it scannable and action-oriented. Lead with the most urgent items.
